Keyboard Controls:
-WASD/Arrow Keys: Move
-Mouse: Aim
-Left Click: Shoot
-Right Click: Melee
-Space: Time Ability (gives new abilities)
-Shift: Dash

Debug Controls:
-L: Reload Level (may cause player to get stuck in terrain)

Gamepad Controls (should be automatically detected):
-Left Joystick: Move
-Right Joystick: Aim
-Right Bumper: Time Ability (gives new abilities)
-Right Trigger: Shoot
-X: Melee
-Left Trigger: Dash
